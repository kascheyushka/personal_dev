About Me

I am a photographer who loves non-standard locations, unique people and sunlight! I specialize in black-and-white photography, but i with no doubts like challenges!

I was born in New York and all since my childhood I liked to watch people in the streets, noticing their features, details and eyes. Now I take their photos both in studio and on the street, and I love them!
